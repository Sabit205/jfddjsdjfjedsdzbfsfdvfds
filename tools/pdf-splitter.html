Frequently Asked Questions

Is this PDF Splitter tool secure to use?

Yes, it is completely secure. Our tool processes your files directly in your web browser. Your PDFs are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring your data remains private and on your own computer.

How do I specify which pages to split?

In the input box, you can define which pages to extract. Enter page numbers or ranges separated by commas. For example, '1-3, 5, 8-10' will create three separate PDF files: one with pages 1 to 3, one with only page 5, and one with pages 8 to 10.

How are the split files downloaded?

All the new PDF files created from your specified ranges will be conveniently bundled and downloaded together in a single .zip file.

Can I split a password-protected PDF?

No, this tool cannot process encrypted or password-protected PDF files due to security restrictions. You will need to remove the password protection from the PDF before you can split it.
