Frequently Asked Questions

What is GST (Goods and Services Tax)?

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is an indirect tax used in many countries on the supply of goods and services. It is a value-added tax levied on most goods and services sold for domestic consumption.

What is the difference between 'Add GST' and 'Remove GST'?

'Add GST' (or exclusive) is used when you have a base price and want to find the final price after tax is included. 'Remove GST' (or inclusive) is used when you have a final price that already includes the tax, and you want to find out what the original base price and the tax amount were.

Is my financial data safe on this website?

Yes, absolutely. This GST calculator performs all calculations directly in your browser. The amounts you enter are never sent to our servers, ensuring your financial information remains private and secure.

How do I use this GST calculator?

First, select whether you want to 'Add GST' to a base price or 'Remove GST' from a final price. Then, enter the amount, select the correct GST rate from the dropdown, and click the 'Calculate' button to see the results.
