Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Text Sorter tool work?

The Text Sorter tool takes the text you provide in the input box, splits it into individual lines, and then rearranges those lines based on the sorting criteria you select (e.g., alphabetical, by length, numerical).

Is my text data safe?

Yes, your data is completely safe. All sorting operations are performed directly in your web browser. Your text is never sent to our servers, ensuring your privacy.

What is the difference between Alphabetical and Numerical sort?

Alphabetical sort treats all lines as text and orders them from A to Z. For example, '10' would come before '2'. Numerical sort attempts to find a number in each line and sorts based on that number's value, so '2' would correctly come before '10'.
