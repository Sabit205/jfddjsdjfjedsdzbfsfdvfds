Frequently Asked Questions

How does this tool remove duplicate lines?

This tool reads all the lines from the input text area and keeps only the first occurrence of each line. Subsequent identical lines are removed, and the cleaned list is displayed in the result area.

Is my data secure?

Yes, your data is completely secure. All processing is done directly in your web browser. Nothing you paste into the text area is ever sent to our servers.

What does 'Case Sensitive' mean?

When 'Case Sensitive' is checked, 'Apple' and 'apple' are treated as two different, unique lines. If it is unchecked, they are considered duplicates, and only the first one encountered will be kept.
