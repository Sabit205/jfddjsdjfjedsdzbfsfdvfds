Frequently Asked Questions

How "random" are these numbers?

This tool uses the browser's built-in Math.random() function, which generates pseudo-random numbers. This is sufficient for most everyday purposes like games, simulations, or picking a random winner. However, it is not suitable for cryptographic applications that require a higher level of security.

What does 'Allow Duplicates' mean?

When 'Allow Duplicates' is checked, each number is generated independently, so the same number can appear more than once in the results. If you uncheck it, the tool will ensure that every number in the generated list is unique. Note that you cannot generate more unique numbers than are available in the specified range.

Can I generate random numbers with decimals?

Yes. Check the 'Allow Decimals' box, and the tool will generate floating-point numbers within your specified range instead of just whole numbers (integers).

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All number generation happens locally in your browser. No settings or generated numbers are sent to our servers, ensuring your privacy.
