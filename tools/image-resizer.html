Frequently Asked Questions

How does the image resizer work?

Our Image Resizer uses JavaScript to perform all operations directly in your web browser. When you upload an image, it's loaded onto a hidden canvas, redrawn to your specified dimensions, and then exported as a new image file that you can download. Your files are never sent to a server.

Is it safe to upload my images here?

Yes, it is completely safe. Since the entire resizing process happens on your computer (client-side), your images are never uploaded to our or any third-party servers. Your privacy is fully protected.

What does 'Maintain aspect ratio' do?

The 'Maintain aspect ratio' lock ensures that your image does not get stretched or distorted. When it's locked, changing the width will automatically update the height to match the original image's proportions, and vice versa. This is recommended for most use cases to keep your images looking natural.

Can I resize multiple images at the same time?

Yes. You can drag and drop multiple images or select them from your computer. The tool will apply the same resizing rules to all images in the queue. After resizing, you can download them individually or all at once in a single .zip file.
