Frequently Asked Questions

What is a case converter?

A case converter is a tool that allows you to change the capitalization of your text. You can instantly switch between formats like all uppercase, all lowercase, title case (where major words are capitalized), and sentence case (where only the first letter of a sentence is capitalized).

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, the NexaTools Case Converter is completely free to use. There are no hidden fees or limitations.

Do you store the text I paste into the converter?

No. For your privacy, all text conversion is done directly in your browser. Your data is never sent to or stored on our servers.
