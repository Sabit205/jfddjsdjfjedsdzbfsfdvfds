Frequently Asked Questions

What is keyword density?

Keyword density is a percentage that represents how frequently a specific keyword or phrase appears in a piece of text compared to the total number of words. It is often used in search engine optimization (SEO) to analyze content relevance.

Why is it important to check keyword density?

Checking keyword density helps you understand if your content is well-optimized for a target keyword without overusing it (known as 'keyword stuffing'). It ensures your text is relevant to search engines and reads naturally to users.

What are 1-word, 2-word, and 3-word phrases?

These are also known as n-grams. A 1-word phrase is a single keyword. A 2-word phrase is a two-word combination (bigram), and a 3-word phrase is a three-word combination (trigram). Analyzing these helps identify common themes and long-tail keywords in your text.
