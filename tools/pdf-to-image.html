Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats can I convert my PDF to?

Our tool allows you to convert your PDF pages into two of the most popular image formats: PNG (ideal for graphics and transparency) and JPG (ideal for photographs).

Is this PDF to Image converter secure?

Yes, absolutely. The entire conversion process happens in your web browser. Your PDF files are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring your data remains private and secure on your own computer.

Can I convert specific pages from the PDF?

Yes! After uploading your PDF, you can specify exactly which pages you want to convert. You can enter single page numbers (e.g., 3), ranges (e.g., 5-8), or a combination of both (e.g., 1, 4, 6-9).

How are multiple images downloaded?

If you convert a single page, it will be downloaded as a single image file (PNG or JPG). If you convert multiple pages, they will be conveniently bundled and downloaded together in a single .zip file.
