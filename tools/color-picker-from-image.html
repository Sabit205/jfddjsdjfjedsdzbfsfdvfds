Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pick a color from my image?

After uploading your image, simply move your mouse cursor over it. The magnified view (loupe) and color codes will update in real-time. Click on any pixel to lock in that color and see its details.

Is my uploaded image secure?

Yes. Your image is processed entirely in your web browser. It is never uploaded to our servers, ensuring your files and data remain private.

What is an image palette?

The image palette is a set of dominant colors automatically extracted from your uploaded image. It gives you a quick overview of the main colors used, which is great for design inspiration.
