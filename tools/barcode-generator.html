Frequently Asked Questions

Which barcode format should I use?

The best format depends on your needs. CODE128 is a versatile choice for alphanumeric data in logistics and inventory. EAN-13 and UPC are standard for retail products sold worldwide and in North America, respectively. CODE39 is an older, widely supported format for simple alphanumeric codes.

Is this barcode generator free to use?

Yes, our barcode generator is completely free for both personal and commercial use. There are no limits on how many barcodes you can create or download.

How do I save the generated barcode?

After generating your barcode, simply click the 'Download as PNG' button. The tool will convert the barcode image into a high-quality PNG file and automatically start the download to your computer.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All barcode generation happens directly in your browser using client-side JavaScript. The data you enter is never sent to our servers, ensuring your information remains private.
