Privacy Policy for NexaTools

Effective Date: October 26, 2023

NexaTools ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit and use our website, NexaTools (the "Service"). Your use of our Service signifies your acceptance of this policy.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

Our core principle is to respect your privacy. We have designed our tools to be as non-intrusive as possible:

  • No Personal Registration Data: We do not require you to create an account or provide any personal information like your name, address, or phone number to use our tools.
  • No Storing of Uploaded Files: When you use our tools to process a file (e.g., PDF to Image, Image Compressor), your files are uploaded to our secure servers for processing. We do not view, copy, or analyze your files in any way. All uploaded files are automatically and permanently deleted from our servers within a short period (typically one hour) after processing is complete.

2. Information We DO Collect

To improve our Service and understand how it is used, we collect limited, non-personally identifiable information:

  • Log Files & Analytics Data: Like most websites, we use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to collect anonymous information. This includes your IP address (often anonymized), browser type, internet service provider (ISP), referring/exit pages, date/time stamps, and the number of clicks. This data is aggregated and used purely for statistical analysis to identify trends, administer the site, and improve user experience. This information is not linked to any individual's identity.

3. Cookies and Web Beacons

A "cookie" is a small text file stored on your computer. We use cookies to enhance your experience.

  • Functional Cookies: We may use cookies to remember your preferences, such as your choice between light and dark mode.
  • Analytics Cookies: Our analytics partners use cookies to gather the anonymous usage data described above.
  • Advertising Cookies: We may use third-party advertising companies, like Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies (such as the DART cookie) to serve ads based on a user's visit to our site and other sites on the Internet. You can opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy.

You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser options. For more detailed information about cookie management with specific web browsers, please refer to the browsers' respective websites.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We use standard security measures, including HTTPS, to protect information during transmission. While no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data during the brief time it is on our servers.

5. Third-Party Links

Our Service may contain links to other websites that are not operated by us. If you click on a third-party link, you will be directed to that third party's site. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to remove it.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
